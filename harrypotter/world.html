Tips for Harry Potter World:

Stay at on of the on-site hotels. There are four you can choose from:

  • Loews Portofino Bay Hotel -- Universal's flagship resort, usually the highest priced but has the largest rooms and most amenities, somewhat removed from the noise of the theme parks.
  • Hard Rock Hotel -- Most popular on-site hotel, right next to Universal Studios Florida, killer pool area.
  • Loews Royal Pacific Resort -- Often the least expensive "luxury" on-site hotel, closest to Islands of Adventure, features a luau Saturday nights and very popular for first-time Universal visitors.
  • Cabana Bay Beach Resort -- This is Universal's value property, offering the lowest on-site rates and also -- a first for the resort -- family suites that sleep up to six people and include a kitchenette.
(http://orlandoinformer.com/page/articles.html/_/universal/harry-potter/harry-potter-world-beat-the-crowds)

If you stay at one of these hotels, you get to access Harry PotterWorld 1 Hour earlier in the Morning, which is VERY important. You can use this time to get ahead in the line for either "Escape from Gringotts" in Diagon Alley in the Universal Studios Florida, or "The Forbidden Jouney" in Hogsmeade in Islands of Adventure.
