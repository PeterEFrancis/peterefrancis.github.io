I am a proud math nerd, programmer, and someone who likes adding third phrases to written lists out of love for the Oxford comma.

I graduated as valedictorian from Gettysburg College in the spring of 2021 with a BA mathematics, and am now a PhD student of Evita Nestoridi at Stony Brook University writing a thesis on separation distance limit profiles of Markov chains.

When I'm not proving things, I enjoy elimintating projects from my mile-long to-do list. These can include math or CS research, programming web tools, composing music, developing games, or writing philosophy and fiction. Of course, not every idea results in a masterpiece, but this website includes the things I am most proud of.

If you are interested in working together, please contact me!
